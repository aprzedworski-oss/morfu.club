About the Creator

Meet the creator of Morfu

Morfu was created by Andrzej as a warm, curious companion who invites children to take part in every adventure — not just watch from the sidelines.

Discover the First BookMeet Morfu

A simple idea

Stories feel more magical when children are part of them.

Morfu’s adventures are designed around shared discovery. Children help find clues, choose paths, notice animals and support Morfu when a challenge feels difficult.

The goal is not to turn a story into a lesson. It is to create a gentle moment of curiosity, kindness and imagination that children and grown-ups can enjoy together.

⭐Every child deserves a place in the adventure.

Interactive Stories

Children look, choose, answer and help Morfu move the story forward.

Real-World Wonder

Animals and places inspire simple discoveries without turning the adventure into a lecture.

Gentle Values

Curiosity, courage, cooperation and kindness grow naturally from the story.

Beyond the Book

Printable Backpack activities give families another way to return to Morfu’s world.

From the creator

Why Morfu?

I wanted to create a character who does not stand above the child or always know the answer. Morfu is curious, sometimes uncertain and always ready to try again with a friend beside him.

That is why every Morfu adventure says the same thing in a different way: we discover more when we explore together.

— Andrzej
Creator of Morfu
