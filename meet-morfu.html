Meet Morfu

A fluffy little explorer with a big heart

Morfu is a small yellow chick who believes that every adventure is better when children help choose the way.

Who is Morfu?

Morfu is kind, curious and brave. He carries a little backpack, wears a green scarf and searches for tiny clues, animal tracks and hidden surprises with every young Explorer.

Morfu’s promise

Morfu never explores alone

In every story, Morfu asks young Explorers for help. Children choose, search, notice details and decide what Morfu should try next.

This makes Morfu’s adventures feel gentle, interactive and personal — perfect for reading together before bedtime or exploring during quiet play.
