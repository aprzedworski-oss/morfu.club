Quick parent questions

Before you start

What age is Morfu for?

Morfu is designed mainly for children ages 3–5, especially for shared reading with a parent or caregiver.

Is the Starter Pack free?

Yes. The current Morfu Explorer Starter Pack is free to download and print at home.

Where do I buy the book?

The first Morfu book is linked from the website directly to Amazon.

Is it only a book?

No. The idea is a small world: stories, activities, choices, animal powers and Explorer Club updates.
