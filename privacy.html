Last updated: July 2026

Morfu.club Privacy Policy

Morfu.club provides children’s book information, printable activity ideas and family-friendly adventure content. Adults may use forms on the site to contact us or join the Explorer Club.

Information we collect

When you submit a form, we may collect the information you provide, such as your name, email address, subject, message or optional Explorer name.

How we use information

We use form information to respond to messages, send occasional Morfu updates, share activity news and improve the website experience.

Children’s privacy

Morfu.club is designed for families and young children, but forms should be completed by an adult. Please do not submit personal information from a child without parent or guardian permission.

Email updates

If you join the Explorer Club, you may receive occasional updates about Morfu books, printable activities and related family-friendly content. You can ask to be removed from the list at any time.

Third-party services

The site may be hosted with Cloudflare. Contact forms and email updates should be completed by adults only. Book links may lead to Amazon pages.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact form.
