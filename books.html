A story with a gentle purpose

Helping someone small can become a very big adventure

Morfu and the Little Octopus blends a simple rescue story with emotional learning. Morfu admits when he is unsure, asks the child for help and discovers that courage does not mean feeling no fear—it means taking the next step together.

  • Encourages empathy and kindness
  • Supports observation and simple decision-making
  • Creates natural conversation during reading
  • Keeps the tone bright, safe and reassuring

Animal Power

Dolphin Power

Morfu remains the same yellow chick Explorer. He only borrows helpful dolphin traits for the ocean mission—and still needs the child’s guidance.

For parents and caregivers

Easy to read aloud. Easy to revisit.

Made for bedtime, quiet afternoons and meaningful family time. The story stays clear and gentle while giving children enough participation to remain involved.
